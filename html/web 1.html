Sustainable Development Goals

also known as the Global Goals. These are a set of 17 universal goals adopted by the United Nations

in 2015 as a call to action to end poverty, protect the planet, and ensure that all people enjoy peace

and prosperity by 2030. The SDGs aim to transform the world and address issues like poverty, inequality,

climate change, and more.

 

SDG 4

'Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all'

is made up of 10 targets and 11 indicators. It covers measures of children and adult education , skills but also gender disparity .

The means of implementation indicators focus on both education facilities for students and on quality of teacher supply.
